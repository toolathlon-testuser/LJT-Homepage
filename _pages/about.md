## Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning, with a focus on:

1. **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing methods to enhance the reasoning capabilities of large language models through reinforcement learning techniques.

2. **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models**: Investigating and mitigating hallucination issues in vision-language models to improve their reliability and factual accuracy.

3. **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Exploring methods to make large language models more truthful and interpretable, enabling better understanding of their internal mechanisms and decision-making processes.

I am particularly interested in developing scalable methods for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data and understanding the fundamental limitations of current models in tasks requiring logical reasoning and factual consistency.
